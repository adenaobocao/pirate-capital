# THE PIRATE CAPITAL: concept, token and narrative

> History: born as "Caveira Capital", briefly "Coco Kennel", now the pirate capital
> (2026-07-22). Same product all along: 4 ai agents trading stock tokens in public,
> plus a support token. The Pirate Bay is the visual and naming reference; the
> plan is real money and a real token, staged (see docs/ROADMAP.md).

## The thesis

Attention without utility dies. The tech is the utility: four ai agents with lives
of their own trading stock tokens 24/7 on the Robinhood Chain narrative, with the
public watching every decision in real time. The ship is the brand; the crew is
the product; the chain is the stage; the token is the flag.

## Naming

- **Site / project: the pirate capital.** The Pirate Bay cadence, finance house
  meaning, and it inherits the "capital" from the original Caveira Capital.
- **Token: $PIRATE.** Loud, obvious, degen native. One word says the whole brand.
- Crew: Flint (captain, contrarian), Cannon (gunner, momentum), Crow (lookout,
  sentiment), Ledger (quartermaster, quant).
- Subsystems: the tavern (crowd sim), a thousand voyages (monte carlo),
  the articles (risk guardrails), the chests (bankrolls), the log (trade feed).
- Discarded token names: $BAG, $BOOTY, $DOUBLOON, $PLANK.

## The token (full design in docs/TOKENOMICS.md)

**$PIRATE on Pons.** Fixed supply, liquidity locked at graduation. Two mechanics,
both publicly traceable end to end:

1. Creator fees from Pons feed the crew's chests (the bankroll grows with volume).
2. When the crew profits big, buy $PIRATE on the open market and burn it,
   tx hash published.

Non negotiable line: it is not a share, gives no rights and promises nothing.
"Watch", never "get rich". Watching stays free forever.

## The product in layers

1. **Watch (free)**: public site with the 4 pirates, equity, trades, reasoning and
   the tavern. Every trade becomes shareable content.
2. **Public wallets (era 2)**: each pirate with its own address on the explorer.
   "Don't trust it? Check the chain."
3. **$PIRATE on Pons (era 3)**: fees in, burns out, all onchain.
4. **Scale (era 4)**: fifth pirate, per pirate pages, whatever holds the code.

## Launch sequence

1. Sea trials: hourly ticks for 2 to 4 weeks, real history accumulating in public.
2. Go loud with the site once the log is alive (nobody watches an empty aquarium).
3. Era 2: real chests, our own money only ($1k per pirate, the Steve model), public wallets.
4. Era 3: $PIRATE on Pons with the site live and the log full. Fees routed, burn policy published.

## Serious warnings (read before era 2 and era 3)

- **An agent trading other people's money = regulated territory** (asset management).
  The chests hold OUR money only, ever. That line never moves.
- Real money agentic trading and token launches have different rules per
  jurisdiction. Lawyer before shipping era 2 and era 3.
- Robinhood stock tokens are not offered in every region. Verify.
- Never promise returns. Not on the site, not in a post, not in a group chat.
  The buyback and burn is stated intent conditional on profits, never a guarantee.
